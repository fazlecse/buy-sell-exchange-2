About

Experience the ultimate in luxury and exclusivity with a private 2-hour yacht cruise along the enchanting Bosphorus Strait. Designed to accommodate up to nine guests, this intimate aquatic adventure is an ideal choice for a memorable family celebration, a romantic evening getaway, or an elegant business gathering.

Indulge in the breathtaking scenery as you glide past iconic landmarks, including the majestic Hagia Sophia, the historic Topkapi Palace, the charming district of Üsküdar, and a host of other architectural marvels that line the strait. The leisurely pace of the cruise allows you to fully immerse yourself in the panoramic views and the unique cultural tapestry of Istanbul.

To enhance your experience, meals can be freshly prepared on board at your request for an additional cost, offering a bespoke dining experience tailored to your preferences. Whether you're savoring a gourmet meal under the open sky or simply enjoying the tranquil waters, this private yacht cruise promises an unforgettable journey through one of the world's most captivating waterways.

Marvel at the iconic Hagia Sophia, where intricate mosaics and stunning architecture narrate nearly 1500 years of history. Note that access for non-Muslims is limited to the gallery area, which may restrict your experience.
