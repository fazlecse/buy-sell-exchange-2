Geçit Art and More

(5)
Art Galleries
Duration: Less than an hour
Overview

Geçit Art & More presents a unique opportunity to explore an extensive collection of art-related products that are hard to find elsewhere in Istanbul. As you wander through the space, you'll discover a diverse array of items that cater to various tastes and interests. The knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist, offering insightful recommendations tailored to your preferences. You'll appreciate the reasonable prices, making it easy to find something special without breaking the bank. This vibrant venue not only showcases creativity but also fosters a welcoming atmosphere, ensuring your experience is both enjoyable and enriching.

Hobyar Mah. Ankara Cad. 11/A, Istanbul Türkiye
