The Historical Peninsula of Istanbul offers a captivating blend of Christian and Islamic heritage. Marvel at the iconic Hagia Sophia, where intricate mosaics and stunning architecture narrate nearly 1500 years of history. While the exterior impresses with its grandeur, the interior reveals a rich tapestry of art and culture, best appreciated with an audio guide. Note that access for non-Muslims is limited to the gallery area, which may restrict your experience. Despite the crowds and occasional construction, the serene atmosphere invites reflection on the coexistence of diverse traditions. Plan your visit wisely to navigate the lines and fully immerse yourself in this enchanting part of Istanbul. - Powered by AI
